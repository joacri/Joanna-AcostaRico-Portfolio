Educational History Game:

Time-Traveling Alien General is a history-themed geography game where players travel through key moments of World War I and World War II to locate relevant countries on a world map. This was the result of a quarter long project for an introduction to software engineering class. Our target audience for this game is high schoolers that are taking a World History class, this game should flesh out their understanding and knowledge of the two World Wars. The user is playing as an alien war general who goes back in time, trying to send their troops to specific locations on Earth. The alien overlord gives them a mission to recreate the events of WWI and WWII.
